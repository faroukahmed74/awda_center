Privacy Policy

Awda Center (Awda Physical Therapy) – Last updated: February 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Awda Center (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile and web application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide and data necessary to run the service:

  • Account information: email address, password (hashed), name (Arabic/English), phone number.
  • Profile and roles: your role (e.g. admin, doctor, secretary, trainee, patient) and permissions.
  • Appointments: booking and scheduling data (date, time, patient, doctor, status).
  • Patient and clinical data: (when applicable) medical history, sessions, documents, and notes you or staff add.
  • Financial records: (for authorized users) income and expense entries, including salary and other categories.
  • Device and usage: we may use push notification tokens and analytics to operate and improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Provide and secure the app (authentication, authorization, and backups).
  • Manage appointments, patient care, and center operations.
  • Process income and expenses for authorized roles.
  • Send you relevant notifications (e.g. appointment reminders) if you have allowed them.
  • Comply with law and enforce our terms.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore). We rely on Firebase’s security and compliance measures. Access inside the app is restricted by role and permissions (e.g. admin, doctor, secretary).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only:

  • With service providers (e.g. Firebase/Google) that help us run the app under strict confidentiality.
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You can update your profile and account details in the app. For other requests or to delete your account, contact us using the details below.

6. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
